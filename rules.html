Guidelines:

  1. Your innovation should focus towards providing novel solutions to any ONE of the key SDG areas floated for the competition.
  2. Your ideas should be original and innovative. Plagiarism of any sort shall not be tolerated.
  3. The Internet can be used as a source of material and information regarding the topic in question.
  4. A certificate of participation will be given to all those participants who submit their ideas within the timeline frame.
  5. Projects will be shortlisted from all submissions and evaluated by external speakers of Xientia based on a selection criterion.
  6. The top three will be selected as the winners, with the following prize distribution:
    1. First Prize: Rs. 5000
    2. Second Prize: Rs. 3000
    3. Third Prize: Rs. 5000
  7. The decision of the judges shall be final and binding.

Who Can Participate?

  1. This Ideathon can be participated in by students of VIT Vellore only.
  2. A team of up to four students can register.
  3. Teams can be from any course and department.

How to apply?

  1. Interested teams may upload their team member details and project online through our website. The link will be shared through the server and on our social media platforms.
  2. Registrations will open from 18th November 2021 and will end on 25th November2021.
  3. One member from a team can register for all the team members.
