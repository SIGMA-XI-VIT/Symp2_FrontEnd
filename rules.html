IDEATHON

Pollution management for sustainable environment

GUIDELINES

1. Your innovation should focus towards providing novel solutions to any ONE of the key SDG areas. The internet may be used a source of information but plagiarism will not be tolerated.

2. A team of up to four students from any department of VIT Vellore can participate.

3. A certificate of participation will be given to all those participants who submit their ideas within the timeline frame. Submissions start from 18th November 2021 and end on 25th November 2021 at 12:00 AM.

4. Interested team members may upload their project in .pptx format (around 20 slides) to a shared drive link accessible for all. It must contain the SDG theme chosen, name of project/idea, name of team and names of team members.

5. Projects will be shortlisted from all submissions and evaluated by external speakers of Xientia based on a selection criterion.

6. The top three will be selected as the winners, with the following prize distribution:

  1. First Prize: Rs. 5000
  2. Second Prize: Rs. 3000
  3. Third Prize: Rs. 2000

For teams of up to four people, the cash prize will be shared amongst the members. One team member’s bank details will have to be shared for the same.

7. The announcement of the winners shall be made during Xientia’s first installment on 27 October 2021, from 12:00 PM to 2:00 PM IST.
