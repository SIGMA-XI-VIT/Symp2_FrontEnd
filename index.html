ABOUT EVENT

Sigma Xi presents Xientia-a symposium on the globally relevant subject: Pollution Detection and Management for Safe Health and Sustainable Environment. The event features 5 esteemed speakers who are resource persons in diverse research domains concerned with Environmental Health and Sustainable Development. Deliberation will be held among the speakers, enlightening the audience about the effects of pollution on health and the environment, and ways to achieve sustainability.

We are also conducting an exciting ‘Ideathon’ on a similar theme to our symposium which is "Pollution Management for Sustainable environment". Participants of the Ideathon will be able to showcase their resolve to tackle this global issue by coming up with sustainability models and stand a chance to win from a prize pool of Rs 10000.
